How to Use EduVerse

This quick guide shows you how to navigate the site, buy textbooks, earn & use cashback, manage assignments, and get help from the AI Study Assistant.

menu

1. Getting Around EduVerse

The top navigation bar helps you move between the main parts of EduVerse:

  • Dashboard: Your home base. See your assignments and quick links to textbooks.
  • Search for Textbooks: Browse, search, and add textbooks to your cart.
  • Cashback / Purchased Textbooks: View past purchases and see your cashback balance.
  • AI Study Assistant: Chat with the AI to break down chapters, concepts, and practice questions.
  • Cart Icon: Opens your cart on the /affordable_textbooks_hub/ page.
person_add

2. Creating an Account & Logging In

Your EduVerse account keeps your assignments, textbooks, and cashback connected to your name.

  1. Go to the EduVerse login/signup page.
  2. Under “New to EduVerse?”, enter your name, email, and password.
  3. Agree to the terms and click Sign Up.
  4. Use your email/username and password in the Login section to sign in.
  5. After logging in, you’re taken to your Student Dashboard (/student_homepage_with_to-do/), which greets you with “Welcome, [Your Name]”.
shopping_bag

3. How to Search & Purchase Textbooks

Use the search page to find affordable textbooks and add them to your cart.

  1. From the top navigation, click Search for Textbooks to open /search_textbooks_hubcode/.
  2. Use the search bar to look up a book by ISBN, title, or author.
  3. Use the filters on the left to narrow by price, condition, and format (e-book, rental, hardcover, etc.).
  4. When you find a book you like, click “Add to Cart”.
  5. Click the shopping_cart cart icon in the top bar to go to Your Cart (/affordable_textbooks_hub/).
  6. On the cart page, you can:
    • Adjust quantities for each textbook.
    • Remove items you no longer want.
    • See your Subtotal, Shipping, Total, and Cashback Earned.
  7. When you’re ready, click “Proceed to Checkout”. After purchase, your books are recorded on the Purchased Textbooks (/purchased_textbooks/) page.
payments

4. How the Cashback System Works

EduVerse gives you cashback credits every time you purchase a textbook. These credits can be used toward future orders.

  1. On the cart page (/affordable_textbooks_hub/), look at the Order Summary panel:
    • Cash Back: shows your current cashback wallet balance.
    • You’re earning 10% cashback! shows how much you’ll earn from this order.
  2. Use the “Use” button next to Cash Back to toggle:
    • Off (“Use”): your cashback wallet is saved for later.
    • On (“Using”): cashback is applied to reduce your total, and a “Cashback Applied” row appears.
  3. After checkout, your cashback history and updated balance are stored and displayed on the Purchased Textbooks (/purchased_textbooks/) page.
  4. You can reuse your cashback on future textbook orders as long as your balance is above $0.

5. How to Add Assignments

Your dashboard keeps track of what’s due so your textbooks and tasks stay in one place.

  1. From the top navigation, go to your Dashboard (/student_homepage_with_to-do/).
  2. Find the Assignments section and click “+ New Assignment”.
  3. In the assignment form, fill in:
    • Course name (e.g., “Macroeconomics”)
    • Assignment title (e.g., “Chapter 3 Problem Set”)
    • Due date and time
    • Optional notes (e.g., “Use textbook pages 40–55”)
  4. Click Save. The assignment will appear in your list and on your dashboard.
  5. In the future, you’ll also be able to extract assignments directly from a syllabus upload and automatically populate this section.

6. How to Use the AI Study Assistant

The AI Study Assistant helps you actually understand the material—not just memorize it.

  1. Click AI Study Assistant in the top navigation to open /ai_study_assistant/.
  2. Tell the AI what you’re working on. Examples:
    • “Explain Chapter 2 from my Intro to Psychology book in simple terms.”
    • “Create 5 practice questions on supply and demand.”
    • “Help me understand this theorem from Calculus: Early Transcendentals.”
  3. You can mention the textbook you purchased, your course name, or specific page numbers so the AI gives targeted help.
  4. Use the responses to build study guides, summarize readings, or prepare for exams.
help

7. If You Get Stuck

If something doesn’t look right—like assignments not saving, cashback not updating, or textbooks not appearing in your cart—try refreshing the page first. If the issue continues, clear your browser’s local storage for this site or contact support through the “Contact” link in the footer.
